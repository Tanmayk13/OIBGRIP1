Cristiano Ronaldo

Portuguese Football Player

Lifetime Timeline Of Cristiano Ronaldo :

  • 1985 - Born in Funchal, Madeira
  • 1995-96 - U-13 Regional Championship
  • 2002 - Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira
  • 2003-04 - FA Cup
  • 2004 - UEFA European Championship runner-up
  • 2004-05 - FA Cup runner-up
  • 2005-06 - Football League Cup
  • 2006 - FIFA World Cup fourth place
  • 2006-07 - Premier League, FA Cup runner-up
  • 2007 - FA Community Shield
  • 2007-08 -Premier League, UEFA Champions League
  • 2008 - FIFA Club World Cup, FA Community Shield
  • 2008-09 - Premier League, Football League Cup, UEFA Champions League runner-up
  • 2010-11 - Copa del Rey, Supercopa de España runner-up, La Liga
  • 2012 - UEFA European Championship bronze medal, Supercopa de España, Copa del Rey runner-up
  • 2013-14 - Copa del Rey, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Super Cup, Supercopa de España runner-up, FIFA Club World Cup
  • 2015-2016 -UEFA Champions League, UEFA European Championship, FIFA Club World Cup, La Liga
  • 2017 - FIFA Confederations Cup third place, UEFA Super Cup, Supercopa de España, FIFA Club World Cup, UEFA Champions League
  • 2018 - Supercoppa Italiana, Serie A, UEFA Nations League
  • 2019 - Supercoppa Italiana runner-up, Coppa Italia runner-up, Serie A
  • 2020 - Supercoppa Italiana, Coppa Italia
  • 2021 - Champions League
  • 2022 - Fifa World Cup 2022-23, Champions League
